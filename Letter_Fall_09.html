PRACTICE

Now is the time to start thinking about when to fit in that regular practice session for each student!  In order to feel successful and to progress students must practice regularly.  Without this students tend to lose interest in their instrument and lessons can become unnecessarily challenging.  Development of a regular practice routine is essential to the development of each young musician.  Students should be practicing a minimum of 5 days each week.  Students taking 30 minute lessons should practice a minimum of 20 minutes a day.  Students taking 45 minute lessons should practice a minimum of 35 minutes each day.  Students taking 60 minute lessons should practice a minimum of 50 minutes each day.  Set a timer at the beginning of each practice session.  Please be sure students mark down their practice time on their assignment sheet each day.  Beginning students will require some help from their parents to reinforce concepts learned at the lessons.  If you have questions about practice please feel free to contact me.

 

Other ways to help students succeed in their piano studies:

Help cultivate a love of music by listening daily to all kinds of music.  Have your radios tuned to the Classical station 89.9 fm www.allclassical.org  and the jazz station  89.1  www.kmhd.org.  Attend live concerts throughout the year.  Dance!  Check out cds and videos at the library.  Watch short videos on Youtube.  Share your own love of music and music making with your family.
